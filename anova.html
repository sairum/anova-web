Rejection criteria (α) Rejection criterium for H0 in main ANOVA tests. For each term (row) in the ANOVA table, if the probability of the F statistic is smaller than this value, it is displayed in emphasized bold font. For each term, you should interpret the probabilities in the ANOVA table as the probability of obtaining an F value equal or larger than the observed F value.
